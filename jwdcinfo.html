JWDC Flyer Concept

Made for the Japan America Society of Washington D.C. to promote Japan bowl. Flyers were one of many presented at meeting. The spirit of the flyer was to show the interconnectedness of the two nations, as well as show a competitive nature and highlight some of the main features of Japan and Washington D.C.

  • Made in Photoshop CS4
  • Made to highlight the relationship between the two nations.
  • Design printed on glossy paper.
